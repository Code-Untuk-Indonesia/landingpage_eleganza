Why ELEGANZA Indonesia?

T. Eleganza Tile Indonesia (“ETI”) was established in 2010. ETI is a subsidiary company of PT Catur Sentosa Adiprana Tbk (ownership percentage of 51%) which supplies high quality porcelain tile, ceramic tile, mosaic, and accessories at affordable prices. With a variety of types ranging from Polished, Unpolished, Matte, Rustic, Semi-polished, and Glass & Stone Mosaic, ETI’s product selections are ideal choices for floor, wall, and other applications. ETI offers a wide variety of products that enable customers develop their ideas for both residential and commercial projects. ETI works closely with international factories to develop these products while managing logistics supply to ETI’s own warehouse. ETI’s warehouse and showroom in Jakarta are equipped with well-trained staffs that are ready to fulfill customers’ needs. In addition, ETI sells through modern retail outlets, such as Mitra10, Dunia Bangunan, Pasar Keramik, Home Multi, and Norton Store, as well as traditional retail outlets.
